Lasagna

Lasagna is a type of pasta dish made with layers of flat noodles and sauce.

Ingredients:

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the ground beef until browned.
  3. Layer noodles, meat, sauce, and cheese in a baking dish.
  4. Bake for 45 minutes or until bubbly.
